Of the 147,689 total students enrolled in Boston’s institutions of higher education for Fall 2017, just over 50,000 or 1:3 are housed on-campus or in university-provided housing (44,189 undergraduates and 6,077 graduates). More than 97,000 (66%) students reside off-campus. Approximately 65,500 or two thirds of off-campus students reside in private housing, of which 50.7% are undergraduates and 49.3% are graduate students.
